Service-Learning Course Project as part of DS 4200 F20: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Cody Dunne, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

This project aims to leverage data that we get from our partner and use this opportunity to provide insight and analysis to help restaurants/businesses to make better informed operational decisions that will be beneficial for their company.
